About Us

Modern Frame Gallery was founded in 2012 with a vision to support contemporary art and new artists in the city. Over the years, it has become a hub for creative expression and cultural exchange. We believe in making art accessible and inspiring to everyone!

Notable Artists

  • Maria Perez: Balletic abstracts with vibrant color flow and movement.
  • Daniel Lee: Urban-inspired sculptures, exploring materials and structure.
  • Anya Singh: Nature-inspired paintings, highlighting calm and light.
  • Chen Wu: Mixed media, integrating cultural stories and digital art.
